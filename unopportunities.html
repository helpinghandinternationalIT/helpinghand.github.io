UN Career Opportunities

The United Nations offers multitudes of job opportunities for it's various agencies for people from all educational backgrounds. The UN being the biggest inter-governmental organization in the world has 196 member countries. The number of national and international employment opportunities that it provides is umpteen, yet, most people fail to secure a place at the UN. People who aspire to work with the UN, fail to do so because of the lack of guidance available in India. There are no institutions that mentor people on how to secure a place at the UN, thus even UN aspirants remain unaware of the opportunities provided by the UN and the application process. Most UN aspirants fail to develop a UN friendly CV, or don't know how to write effective cover letters, reducing their chances of selection. As a matter of fact 98% job applications get rejected at the first level of selection. To reduce this disparity, HHI hosts India's First UN Mentorship Program to connect you with the UN. HHI is the first and only Indian organization to mentor students on how to secure a place with the UN.Some of the latest job opportunities posted by the UN are enlisted below. Through India's First UN Mentorship Program, we enable you to connect with UN insiders and learn how to grab these opportunities and make an amazing career with the UN.

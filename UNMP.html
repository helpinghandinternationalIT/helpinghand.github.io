About Program

The United Nations Organization posts numerous job opportunities for it’s various agencies every now and then and even though a lot of people aspire to join the UN very few Indians get a chance to work with it. This is because of the lack of knowledge and guidance available in India. Most people are unaware of the application procedure, and often fail to develop a UN friendly CV, which reduces their chances for selection. As a matter of fact, 98% job applications are rejected in the first level of selection. To reduce this disparity and to help connect more people with the United Nations, Helping Hand International has taken the initiative of hosting India’s first and only UN Mentorship Program, wherein UN insiders train you on how to secure a job with the United Nations. Till now, HHI has been successful in conducting three editions of India’s First UN Mentorship Program, and is currently conducting the fourth one after receiving multiple requests from it's members.

Once you join India’s First UN Mentorship Program, you get eligible for availing a lifetime membership card, through which you can get access to all our webinars and workshops on the United Nations, additionally HHI will keep you posted on any new job opportunity listed by the UN.

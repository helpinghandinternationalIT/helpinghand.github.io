Developing Next Generational Leaders


We work to make the next generation leaders through our education initiatives, internships, summits and conferences, so that they can become a hallmark of change and use these opportunities as a springboard to become future leaders and make this world a better place to live in.

It is proudly announcement that Helping Hand International once again ever made History in Uzbekistan " International Youth offline leadership Campaign 2021" with like-minded youth across the global. The leadership provided an opportunity to ramp up international commitment to the 2030 Agenda, showcased areas of global progress towards the SDGs, and raised awareness on the importance of the 2030 Agenda and different type of activity. The Summit helped leverage progress in key areas to kick-start an acceleration process of social imapact and A challenge for mobilizing a better world.

Helping Hand International has taken a step ahead in bringing together the youth globally for the establishment of UN SDGs.
